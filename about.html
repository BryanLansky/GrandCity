About Grand City



Grand City is a mall that provides shopping centers, restaurants, and various entertainment options located in West Jakarta, DKI Jakarta. Lansky Mall is a project of the Lansky Group which was built on March 2, 2022 and completed on May 29, 2022. Lansky Mall consists of 3 floors where the first floor is a shopping center, the second floor is a collection of restaurants, and the third floor provides various entertainment options with total area of 150,000 square meters.
